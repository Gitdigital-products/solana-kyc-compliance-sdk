Solana KYC Compliance SDK

A privacy-first compliance toolkit for Solana applications. Built to help Web3 teams meet KYC & AML expectations without sacrificing decentralization.

Why this exists

Solana apps are moving from experiments to infrastructure. That means compliance can’t be an afterthought. This SDK gives builders a modular way to integrate KYC-aware flows while keeping user data minimized and off-chain.

Core principles

Privacy-First by Design

No on-chain PII. Identity proofs are abstracted, scoped, and verifiable.

Composable & Modular

Integrates cleanly with existing Solana programs and off-chain services.

Jurisdiction-Aware

Designed to support region-specific compliance logic without forking core code.

Grant-Ready Architecture

Documented, auditable, and structured for ecosystem funding and review.

Open-source infrastructure focused on real-world Web3 adoption.
