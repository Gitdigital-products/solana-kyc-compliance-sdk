Support Wall

Supporters are individuals and organizations who contribute to the project in any meaningful way—financially, through code, documentation, design, governance, translation, mentorship, or community moderation.

We recognize all forms of support.

Type of Support Recognition
Financial Listed in sponsor tiers
Code Listed in contributors wall
Documentation Listed in contributors wall
Design Listed in contributors wall
Governance Listed in contributors wall
Mentorship Listed in contributors wall
Community Moderation Listed in contributors wall

If you have contributed and do not see your name where it belongs, please reach out to the governance team.
