Supporter Community Charter — RickCreator87 Ecosystem

A shared agreement that defines how supporters engage with the ecosystem.

---

1. Mission Alignment
Supporters uphold the mission of:
- transparency  
- governance alignment  
- public‑good infrastructure  
- community empowerment  

---

2. Respect & Conduct
Supporters:
- treat others with respect  
- engage constructively  
- avoid harassment or hostility  
- uphold community values  

---

3. Participation
Supporters may:
- join discussions  
- provide feedback  
- share ideas  
- celebrate releases  

Participation is optional and never required.

---

4. Recognition
Supporters are recognized:
- fairly  
- transparently  
- according to tier and loyalty  

---

5. Independence
Supporters understand:
- sponsorship does not grant governance influence  
- decisions follow documented processes  
- the ecosystem remains founder‑led  

---

6. Amendments
This charter may evolve to strengthen community alignment.
`
