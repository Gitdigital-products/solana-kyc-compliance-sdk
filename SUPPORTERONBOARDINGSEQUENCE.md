Supporter Onboarding Sequence — RickCreator87 Ecosystem

A consistent, founder‑grade onboarding flow for new supporters.

---

1. Welcome Message (Immediate)
Thank you for supporting the mission.  
Your contribution strengthens transparent, auditable, public‑good infrastructure.

---

2. Tier Badge Delivery (Within 24 hours)
Send the appropriate badge (SVG or emoji).  
Add supporter to the Supporter Wall.

---

3. Ecosystem Orientation (Day 2)
Provide links to:
- Sponsorship Profile  
- Supporter Dashboard  
- Roadmap  
- Governance docs  

---

4. Optional Spotlight (Week 1)
Offer to feature the supporter on:
- Ecosystem Sponsors section  
- Badge Wall  
- GitDigital broadcast surfaces  

---

5. Quarterly Check‑In
Share:
- Analytics report  
- New releases  
- Governance updates  
- Creative surfaces added  

---

6. Annual Appreciation
Send a personalized thank‑you message and updated certificate.
`
