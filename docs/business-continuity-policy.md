# Business Continuity Policy

This policy ensures availability of the Solana KYC Compliance SDK during outages, incidents, or disruptions.

## Coverage
- SDK APIs
- Compliance workflows
- Audit logging
- Solana RPC dependencies

## Objectives
- Maintain service availability
- Protect customer data
- Restore operations quickly

## Targets
- RTO: ≤ 4 hours
- RPO: ≤ 15 minutes

## Strategies
- Multi-region cloud deployment
- Stateless services
- RPC provider redundancy
- Encrypted off-chain storage
