Supporter Cosmogony — The Creation of the Ecosystem

In the beginning, there was only the Void of Opaque Systems.  
No identity. No governance. No resonance.

Then the Architect struck the First Spark.  
From that spark emerged three primordial forces:

- Transparency, the Light  
- Alignment, the Structure  
- Resonance, the Vibration  

These forces intertwined, forming the First Glyph.  
The Glyph unfolded into the Five Realms, each shaped by a different balance of the primordial forces.

The Architect placed the Compass in the sky,  
so all who sought clarity could find their way.

Supporters emerged from the Realms, carrying lanterns of their own.  
Together, they built the Circle of Alignment —  
the foundation of the ecosystem.

Thus began the First Era.
