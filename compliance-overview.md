# Compliance Overview

This SDK provides a standardized, verifiable, and jurisdiction‑aware compliance layer for Solana applications.

---

## Supported Compliance Domains

### 1. Identity Verification
- Government ID checks  
- Liveness detection  
- Fraud prevention  

### 2. Sanctions Screening
- OFAC  
- UN  
- EU  
- UK  
- APAC regional lists  

### 3. Age Verification
- 13+  
- 18+  
- 21+ (US)  

### 4. Jurisdiction‑Specific Rules
- US MSB guidance  
- EU AMLD5/6  
- APAC regional KYC rules  
- LATAM fintech requirements  

---

## Compliance Modes

### **Basic**
- Identity + sanctions  
- Suitable for DeFi access control  

### **Enhanced**
- Additional fraud checks  
- Suitable for regulated apps  

### **Enterprise**
- Full AML/KYC suite  
- Suitable for exchanges, fintech, and RWA platforms  

---

## Why This Matters

- Grants often require compliance readiness  
- Users expect safety  
- Regulators expect controls  
- Partners expect due diligence
