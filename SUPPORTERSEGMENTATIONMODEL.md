Supporter Segmentation Model — RickCreator87 Ecosystem

A structured model for categorizing supporters based on behavior, tier, and engagement.

---

Segments

1. New Supporters
- Joined < 30 days  
- High onboarding priority  

2. Active Supporters
- Regular engagement  
- Reads newsletters  
- Visits dashboard  

3. High‑Value Supporters
- Tier ≥ Ecosystem  
- High recognition priority  

4. At‑Risk Supporters
- No engagement in 60–90 days  
- Trigger retention workflow  

5. Advocates
- Shares ecosystem  
- Recommends sponsorship  
- High community alignment  

---

Data Inputs
- Tier  
- Engagement events  
- Newsletter opens  
- Dashboard visits  
- Contribution history  

---

Outputs
- Personalized messaging  
- Spotlight eligibility  
- Retention actions  
`
