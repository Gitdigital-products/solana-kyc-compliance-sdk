Supporter Constitution — RickCreator87 Ecosystem

A foundational document outlining the rights, responsibilities, and cultural principles of all supporters.

---

Article I — Mission
The ecosystem exists to build transparent, auditable, public‑good infrastructure across identity, governance, compliance, and creative systems.

---

Article II — Rights of Supporters
Supporters have the right to:
- transparent documentation  
- fair recognition  
- equal treatment within tiers  
- access to their benefits  
- privacy and data minimalism  

---

Article III — Responsibilities of Supporters
Supporters are expected to:
- uphold community values  
- respect others  
- engage constructively  
- honor the governance charter  

---

Article IV — Independence
Sponsorship does not grant:
- governance influence  
- roadmap control  
- privileged access  
- decision‑making authority  

---

Article V — Cultural Principles
- Transparency  
- Integrity  
- Community alignment  
- Public‑good orientation  
- Founder‑led mission  

---

Article VI — Amendments
This constitution may evolve to strengthen alignment and clarity.
